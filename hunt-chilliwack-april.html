Make sure to bring:

These are self-reliant and self-sufficient hunts so you'll want to bring everything you need to live outside for a few days like food, water and shelter. Some important things not to forget for this hunt are:

- Something to sleep in (Tent, bivy, etc.)
- Pair of dry clothes
- Gun and ammunition
- Rain gear to stay dry
- Gun license (PAL)
- Emergency survival kit
- Hunting tags
